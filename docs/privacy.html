Privacy Policy

Multiply Rush is designed for offline-first play with a minimal data footprint. This policy describes how the game handles information.

Effective date: February 23, 2026 Applies to: Multiply Rush mobile builds

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through the Game.

  • No account registration is required.
  • No analytics or ad tracking SDKs are used in normal gameplay.
  • No in-app purchase SDK is used.

The Game may store local settings and progression on your device (for example, options and gameplay state). This data remains on your device unless you remove app data or uninstall the app.

2. How Information Is Used

Local data is used only to operate game features such as saved preferences and progression. It is not transmitted to us by the Game.

3. Third-Party Services

Multiply Rush is intended to run without third-party advertising, analytics, or in-app commerce services. If this changes in a future release, this page will be updated before that release.

4. Network Use

Multiply Rush is designed for offline gameplay and does not require an active internet connection for core gameplay.

5. Children's Privacy

Because personal information is not collected through the Game, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you remove it, such as by deleting app data or uninstalling the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when product behavior changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/leggoboyo/MultiplyRush/issues .
